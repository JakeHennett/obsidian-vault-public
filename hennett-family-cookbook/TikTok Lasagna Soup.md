# Ingredients
* 19 oz ground Italian sausage
* 23.9 oz spaghetti sauce
* 1 box 32 oz chicken broth
* 1 box 32 oz beef broth
* 16 oz box lasagna noodles
* 1 can 14.5 oz diced Italian tomatoes
* 15 oz Ricotta Cheese
* 8 oz Italian style blend cheese
* 1 Cup Heavy Cream
* Seasonings
	* Seasoning salt
	* Garlic pepper
	* Oregano
	* Basil
# Directions
1. Brown sausage and drain
2. Add spaghetti sauce and both broths
3. Crush noodles and add
4. Add diced tomatoes and season liberally
5. Bring to a boil
6. Submerge noodles, cover, simmer 8 minutes
7. On the side, mix cheeses in a bowl + seasoning
8. After simmer, add some cheese mixture and heavy cream
9. Cook down for 2 more minutes
10. Serve accented with cheese mixture
